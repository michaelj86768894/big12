Week 7 Preview

The Senior Bowl, The Unc Bowl, The Icy-Hot Bowl, etc...

Tonight's matchup between the Browns and the Steelers will be historic -- there has only been one other pairing of 40 year olds to play one another in the Super Bowl Era: Tom Brady & Drew Brees.

It may technically be number two in terms of the combined age of QBs, but it's definitely number one in terms of Old Man Vibes. To me, neither Tom Brady nor Drew Brees ever gave off Old Man Energy the way that, say, Peyton Manning did in his final seasons. The same cannot be said of Joe Flacco, who has seemed old for the last six or seven seasons (six, seven!). This is the ultimate old man matchup and if these two can both end the game without injury, we'll call it a win.

Buffalo & Baltimore are the only two NFL teams on bye this week but those teams have an outsize impact on fantasy matchups (or at least they were expected to before Baltimore decided to not be good at football this season). Byemageddon will occur next week with six teams getting a week off.

It’s Week 7, and we’ve got some juicy storylines brewing across the league. Tucson heads to Milwaukee riding a four-game winning streak, and they’ve owned this matchup 13–7 all-time — Milwaukee’s gonna need more than home-field advantage to flip that script. In Philly, the Soul are hoping to kick Miami while they're down, but the Inferno have taken the last two and lead the series 14–8. Out west, L.A. looks to keep rolling with two straight wins as they square off against Northern Arizona, a team they’ve handled 12–6 over the years. And finally, Vegas will try to keep its two-game streak alive against Cedar Rapids, they’ve owned this series 15–8 and would love nothing more than to get this season back on track.

Streaks are on the line, rivalries are heating up, and it’s time to find out who’s for real — so buckle up, it’s Week 7.
