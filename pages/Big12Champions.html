Since its inception in 2012, the Big 12 Championship has been a battleground for fantasy football excellence, witnessing the crowning of six different teams as champions. Leading the charge with a remarkable three championships are the Miami Inferno, etching their name in the annals of Big 12 history.

The Big 12 recognizes and celebrates outstanding contributions with the following prestigious awards presented annually:

1. General Manager of the Year:

Awarded to the General Manager whose team boasts the best record in the league. This accolade reflects strategic prowess, astute decision-making, and the ability to assemble a winning roster.

2. MVP (Non-QB):

Bestowed upon the player who accumulates the most fantasy points for the season, excluding those in the quarterback position. This award shines a spotlight on exceptional performance, highlighting the impact of players across various positions.

3. Division Champions:

Recognizing the triumph of divisional prowess, this award is granted to the teams that emerge victorious within their respective divisions. It signifies not only individual success but also the ability to navigate the season-long challenges posed by divisional competition.
