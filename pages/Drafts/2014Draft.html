  • Draft

The 2014 draft marked the league's 3rd annual gathering of GLFL General Managers to draft eligible football players. The event unfolded in Wauwatosa, WI, at the former headquarters of Vegas on September 5, 2014, commencing at 4:00 pm CST. The honor of the first overall pick belonged to the Vegas Knight Hawks, who opted to select Adrian Peterson from Minnesota as their top choice. Notably, this marked the second consecutive year that Peterson was chosen with the coveted 1st overall pick.
