The 2021 GLFL draft marked the 9th annual gathering of General Managers to strategically select eligible football players. Departing from traditional in-person drafts, the event took place online on Sunday, September 5, 2021, at 7:00 pm CST. The Vegas Knight Hawks were granted the honor of the first overall pick, and they chose Davante Adams from the Packers, making him the top selection in this year's draft.
