  • Draft

The 2015 draft marked the league's 4th annual gathering of GLFL General Managers, converging at Kam's Corner Tap in Wauwatosa, WI, on September 5th, 2015, at 5:00 pm CST. This draft introduced notable changes, including the implementation of a lottery system on a separate day preceding the actual draft and the inclusion of the option to trade draft picks. The Brooklyn Bolts secured the coveted first overall pick and opted for Eddie Lacy from the Packers to kick off the selections. This draft format added an extra layer of strategy and excitement for both teams and fans alike.
