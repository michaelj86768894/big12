The 2013 draft marked the second annual gathering of GLFL General Managers for the selection of eligible football players. This pivotal event unfolded at Buffalo Wild Wings on Sunday, September 1, 2013, at 4:00 pm CST. Notably, the Miami Inferno secured the honor of holding the first overall pick in the draft, and they strategically chose the reigning MVP, Adrian Peterson, from Minnesota to bolster their roster for the upcoming season. The selection set the tone for an eventful draft, shaping the competitive landscape of the league as teams aimed to strengthen their lineups with top-tier talent.
