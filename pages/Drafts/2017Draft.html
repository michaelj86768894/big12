The 2017 draft marked the 6th annual gathering of GLFL General Managers to select eligible football players. Hosted in Wauwatosa, WI, at Kam's Corner Tap on Sunday, August 20, 2017, at 6:00 pm CST, the draft saw the Northern Arizona Wranglers holding the first overall pick. With this selection, they opted for Antonio Brown from the Pittsburgh Steelers, making it the second time this talented receiver was chosen as the first pick in the draft.
