Since its inception in 2012, the Big 12 has been home to a dynamic array of General Managers, contributing to the league's rich history and competitive spirit. Over the years, a total of 28 different individuals have taken on the role of General Manager, each leaving their unique mark on the league.

Remarkably, four General Managers have remained steadfast since the league's formation in 2012, embodying a deep commitment to the Big 12. Brian Simmons, Cory Colvin, Darrien Clay, and Michael Bautch stand as enduring pillars of the league, demonstrating unwavering dedication and a passion for fantasy football that has spanned the entirety of the Big 12's storied history.

This table serves as a snapshot of the diverse and dedicated individuals who have shaped the narrative of the Big 12, fostering a community of fantasy football enthusiasts and creating lasting memories within the league's vibrant history.
