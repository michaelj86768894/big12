Week 10 Preview

Week 10 is here and it's officially time for everyone to complain about bye weeks -- a thing that definitely could not have been predicted when assembling your team. The good news is that Week 10 has more teams on bye weeks than any other week this season. Those teams are:

  • Cardinals
  • Lions
  • Jaguars
  • Raiders
  • Rams
  • Seahawks

I once again would like to remind the NFL of my idea for two universal bye weeks during the season. Not only would this give time for players to recuperate from injuries, it would -- perhaps more importantly -- give the people in our lives two weekends during the season where we are available to do something other than sit on the couch all day and watch football. Here's the real genius in my idea -- we keep it to ourselves. Nobody has to know. You suddenly call up someone you've been neglecting for two months and say "Hey, I've missed you. Let's get together this weekend and talk about life."

Weeks 7 or 8 are the obvious choices for the First Universal Bye. It's fall! You know what would be a thrill? Going apple picking or going to the pumpkin patch. Again, the critical part here is that you get credit for doing this during the season because nobody but us knows about The Universal Bye. It's a ruse on Non Ball Knowers everywhere. Broadcasters will air re-runs of old games...NBKs won't know!

Let's go ahead and pencil in the weekend of October 24th & 25th as the 2026 Universal Bye. Plan a trip! Book a wedding venue! You can do anything you want, for it is the Universal Bye!

We've got Vikings and Chargers on TNF, which has been surprisingly great this season.

I am still locked into the opinion that Thursday Night Football in the NFL is a dumb idea and makes for bad football, mainly because this game between the Colts and Broncos in 2022 when the final score was 12-9 is what sticks out to me. Will I accept new information, like the quality of this year's games, and update my opinion accordingly? No! Never! TNF sucks!
