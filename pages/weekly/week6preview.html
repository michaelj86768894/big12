Week 6 Preview

Saquon Barkley is no Taylor Swift

We've got the Eagles and Giants for the Thursday night game tonight. The first thought that popped into my head was a dumb one: "Saquon Revenge Game". No, not really. When you're on top of the world, there's really no reason to harbor ill will towards people in your past. Let go of it -- you'll be a happier person. That leads me into the Taylor Swift discourse. Skip the next paragraph if you do not care.

I've got a few problems with "Actually Romantic", the Charli XCX diss track off Taylor's new album. First, she's responding to a song that most would read as primarily self critical and not an actual diss. Second, you can't release a "response" diss track a year and a half after the original diss especially when the theme of the diss is "lol, I'm actually not upset at all". Third, if you are going to wait that length of time, you better be damn sure the song is better than the original. It simply is not better than Sympathy Is A Knife (IMO) -- you gotta pick a better and easier target. This is why Kendrick Lamar is the smartest person in the music industry.

The Broncos and the Jets are the early London game on Sunday. It's been a pretty brutal set of games for our friends overseas so far this season. We need to send a better product over there -- this would be like trying to convince someone that American food is good by taking them to Ruby Tuesday's. Nobody wants to see those nasty little ham chunks at the salad bar -- and they don't want to watch the New York Jets attempt to play football either. Disgusting.
