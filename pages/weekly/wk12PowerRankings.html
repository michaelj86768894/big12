Big 12 Week 12 Power Rankings 2025: How all 12 teams stack up

Week 11 of the 2025 Big 12 season is officially over. This weekend didn't feature any close matchups, going into Monday night every matchup was sitting at either 100-0 or 99-1 win % chances. Now one of those matchup flipped but still, it began 99-1.

How do all these blowout loser fit in our updated Power Rankings? We restacked the Big 12 heading into Week 12, which includes some pretty compelling divisional matchups and not to mention playoff positioning.

Let's get into it, you shouldn't be surprised at our top-3 as they've been up there in some order pretty much all season. Our Power Formula of (Points Scored x2) + (Points Scored * Winning %) + (Points Scored * Winning % if played vs the median score of the week) evaluated how Big 12 teams stack up against one another, ranking them from 1 to 12.
