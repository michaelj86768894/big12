Big 12 Week 11 Power Rankings 2025: How all 12 teams stack up

Week 10 of the Big 12 season featured some exciting finishes, including comebacks in the second half by the Slaughter and Soul on Monday.

How do these teams fit in our updated Power Rankings? We restacked the Big 12 heading into Week 11, which includes byes for the Colts and Saints, congrats in advance to the Knight Hawks for getting Boston at this perfect time (hint: they still wont take advantage of it and lose by 20).

Let's get into it with the No. 1 team, which shifted again from last week. Our Power Formula of (Points Scored x2) + (Points Scored * Winning %) + (Points Scored * Winning % if played vs the median score of the week) evaluated how Big 12 teams stack up against one another, ranking them from 1 to 12.
