Week 4 Preview

The Ryder Cup is finally here. And football! And baseball too!

Hell of a weekend of sports coming up. In a very particular order, we've got:

  1. The Ryder Cup. I know this is a football website but I don't really care. The Ryder Cup is (I'm told) the best sporting event that happens every 2 years & it's not even close.
  2. Ravens vs. Chiefs. We've got two 1-2 teams prepping for absolute battle. One of these two Super Bowl contenders is going home 1-3!
  3. Packers vs. Cowboys. Before we lost CeeDee, the Cowboys were going to get beat by more than 21 and that was me giving the Cowboys more credit than they probably deserve.
  4. The postseason is just around the corner. Brewers I think still need to clinch the top seed? Or maybe they did already, I'm not sure. Everything else is up and down, one day teams control their own destiny. The next they don't. The Hunt for Red October is in full swing (is that what they call the playoff race in baseball?)!
    1. I'mma keep it real with you - I don't care about baseball. Like at all.
