Week 5 Preview: Byes Are Here

Brace yourselves. The bye weeks have arrived.

Week 5 means one thing: bye weeks have arrived. The NFL simply didn't consider the horrific impact that bye weeks would have on fantasy football. Well, I'm a solutions guy, so I have solved it.

The NFL would absolutely never go for it but I am proposing an All-Star week in the middle of the season. No sport needs recovery from injuries more than football -- this would give players a chance to rest and recuperate in whatever ways they see fit. Maybe it's a darkness retreat, eating a bunch of fettucine alfredo to lower chances of concussions, or the best option: Matthew Stafford's God Chamber.

If there were two all star breaks during the season, perhaps we could altogether avoid the fantasy football disaster that is bye weeks. Free idea for the first all-star break: alumni game. Full pads & contact. Don't we all want a chance to see Ray Lewis destroy Tom Brady? Give the fans what they want.

This is always the week when I start to hate my past self more than usual. During the draft, I scoff at people who care about bye weeks...and every year, I play the victim every week that I have multiple players on bye as if I had no control over that setup.
