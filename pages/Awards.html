The GLFL recognizes and celebrates outstanding contributions with the following prestigious annual awards:

1. Most Valuable Player (MVP: Presented to the player who accumulates the most fantasy points during the season, excluding quarterbacks. This award highlights exceptional performance across all positions, adding an extra layer of competition as players vie for recognition as the league’s most impactful performer.

2. General Manager of the Year (GMoY): Awarded to the General Manager whose team achieves the best record in the league. This accolade reflects strategic prowess, astute decision-making, and the ability to assemble a winning roster.
