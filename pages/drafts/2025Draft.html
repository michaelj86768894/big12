The 2025 Big 12 Draft kicked off Monday, September 1st at 7:30 PM—marking the final draft of the re-draft era before our transition to dynasty in 2026. Draft nomination order was determined randomly one hour before draft time, giving Los Angeles the #1 nomination and Vegas the final nomination of the first round. The night delivered everything you’d expect: bold moves, surprise reaches, and a few stolen gems—a fitting finale to over a decade of re-draft tradition.
