The 2020 season would have marked the 9th season in the history of the Big 12. However, the pervasive impact of the Covid-19 pandemic posed considerable challenges for the league. With uncertainties surrounding the safety and well-being of players and staff, coupled with the logistical challenges posed by the pandemic, the league faced difficult decisions. Commissioner Michael Bautch, addressing these challenges during a press conference on July 23, 2020, made the somber announcement that the 2020 season would be canceled. The decision reflected the league's prioritization of the health and safety of all involved during these unprecedented times.
