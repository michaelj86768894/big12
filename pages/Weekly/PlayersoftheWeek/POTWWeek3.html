Gladiators RB De'Von Achane, Sugar Skulls RB Jahmyr Gibbs highlight Week 3 Players of the Week

Jahmyr Gibbs
RB - Tucson Sugar Skulls

In an offensive extravaganza Sunday in which the Lions throttled the Washington Commanders, Gibbs was a dynamo, stacking up three total touchdowns and 172 scrimmage yards in the 44-22 win. Gibbs had a 14-yard touchdown catch to begin the game's scoring -- one of his three catches for 30 yards. He added a 13-yard rushing score and a highlight-reel 43-yard TD as part of a 142-yard rushing effort on just 15 carries -- good for a whopping 9.5 yards per carry. This performance propelled the Tucson Sugar Skulls to victory over the Brooklyn Bolts.

Jessie Bates III
S - Milwaukee Mustangs

Bates was all over the field during the game against the Indianapolis Colts, which the Falcons lost in overtime 31-25. In addition to his interception in the second quarter, he also stripped the quarterback in the first quarter, though the Colts recovered the loose ball. His nine tackles were the second-highest on the team for the week. This performance tallied 23.25 fantasy points for the Milwaukee Mustangs.

De'Von Achane
RB - Cleveland Gladiators

Fantasy owners were shocked by the De'Von Achane game against Buffalo. Achane was a one man wrecking crew as he ended up with 225 yards from scrimmage and two scores in the 30-13 upset of the Bills. Achane is Miami's best offensive player by far and away as he rose above the 40 fantasy point plateau pushing the Cleveland Gladiators passed

Zaire Franklin
LB - Philadelphia Soul

Franklin tallied 10 tackles (six solo), including 1.0 sacks, during the Colts' 31-25 overtime win over the Falcons on Sunday. Franklin saved his biggest play of Sunday's game for last after sacking Michael Penix for a 12-yard loss in overtime, which forced the Falcons to punt on the next play. Sunday marked just the first time this season that the veteran linebacker tallied double-digit tackles leading to a solid 16 fantasy points for the Philadelphia Soul.
