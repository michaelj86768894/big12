Week 10 Preview

There is a crisis of confidence in fantasy football. It seems like everywhere you turn, there's someone complaining about their horrible luck -- whether it be injury, matchup, or some third thing. We must return to being a community of trash talkers. I don't want to see two mopers face off against one another...fantasy football is a fundamentally unserious endeavor. It ought to be treated much like wrasslin. Adopt a persona if you must...but no more of this "poor lil ol' me" stuff. If your team is bad, simply pretend that it isn't and tell your opponent how bad you're gonna whoop their ass.

I gotta tell y'all, it is a great feeling actually looking forward to watching my team play every week. I'm not talking about my fantasy team -- even though i do enjoy beating everyone's ass recently! No, no...I am speaking of the Dallas Cowboys. Even though I know we're going to most likely lose, I still watch and talk shit about how good they are (they're not).

That's the kind of energy we need from this league. If you're sitting at a 1% chance to make the playoffs, you need to believe that you are going to get it done, here's looking at you Dave.

Alright let's get into the only matchups anyone actually cares about this week.
